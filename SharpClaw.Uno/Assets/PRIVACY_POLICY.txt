    read and understood this Privacy Policy.

3.  DATA WE COLLECT AND PROCESS

    SharpClaw does NOT operate any remote backend server, cloud
    service, or remote database. The Application runs a local-only
    service process on your device (bound to localhost) to manage its
    data; this process does not accept connections from the network
    and is not accessible to other devices.

    ALL data generated or entered while using the Application —
    including but not limited to:

        •  user credentials and authentication tokens,
        •  chat history and conversation threads,
        •  agent configurations and system prompts,
        •  API keys for third-party AI providers,
        •  local application settings and preferences,

    — is stored EXCLUSIVELY on YOUR device, in local storage (on-device
    files and/or an in-memory database with local JSON persistence).

    We do not collect, transmit, receive, or store any personal data on
    our own servers. We have no access to your data.

4.  THIRD-PARTY AI PROVIDERS

    SharpClaw allows you to connect to third-party Artificial
    Intelligence ("AI") service providers of your choosing (e.g.
    OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Mistral, Groq, and others).

    When you configure a provider and send chat messages, the content of
    those messages — which may include personal data — is transmitted
    directly from your device to the provider's servers using the API
    key you supply.

    SharpClaw acts solely as a local client interface in this context.
    We do not receive, store, or otherwise process this data on our own
    systems. Each third-party provider acts as an independent data
    controller for the data it receives.

    You are responsible for reviewing and accepting each provider's
    Privacy Policy and Terms of Service before use, and for
    understanding how that provider processes your data.

5.  DATA PROCESSING — LEGAL BASIS (GDPR Art. 6)

    To the limited extent that personal data is processed locally by the
    Application on your device:

        •  Performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR) — processing is
           necessary to provide the Application's core functionality,
           including storing and displaying user data locally.

        •  Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR) — processing is
           necessary to ensure the Application functions correctly,
           including maintaining settings and preferences between
           sessions. These interests do not override your fundamental
           rights, as all data remains under your control on your device.

    We do not process personal data on our own servers.

6.  DATA TRANSFERS

    SharpClaw itself does NOT transfer any personal data to us or to any
    server we control.

    When you choose to connect to a third-party AI provider, data may be
    transferred to servers located outside the EU/EEA (for example, in
    the United States). Such transfers are governed entirely by the
    third-party provider's own privacy policy and applicable safeguards
    (e.g. Standard Contractual Clauses or adequacy decisions).

7.  DATA RETENTION

    All data is stored locally on your device for as long as you choose
    to keep it.

    You may delete any or all Application data at any time by:
        •  using the Application's built-in settings/reset functions;
        •  deleting the Application's local data folder; or
        •  uninstalling the Application.

    We retain no data, as we do not operate server-side storage.

8.  YOUR RIGHTS UNDER THE GDPR AND CROATIAN LAW

    Under the General Data Protection Regulation (EU) 2016/679 ("GDPR")
    and applicable Croatian law, you have the following rights:

        •  Right of access (Art. 15 GDPR)
        •  Right to rectification (Art. 16 GDPR)
        •  Right to erasure (Art. 17 GDPR)
        •  Right to restriction of processing (Art. 18 GDPR)
        •  Right to data portability (Art. 20 GDPR)
        •  Right to object (Art. 21 GDPR)
        •  Right not to be subject to automated decision-making (Art. 22)

    Because we do not have access to or store your personal data, we are
    generally not in a position to fulfill such requests directly.
    However, you can exercise full control over your data locally on your
    device as described in Section 7.

9.  CHILDREN'S PRIVACY
